Sci-fi Corridor

A dim, futuristic sci-fi corridor glows with flickering neon—blue and orange lights casting sharp reflections on rusted metal walls. The space blends sleek tech with decayed industry. At its center hangs a human figure, missing a left leg and half of the other, suspended in silence—a grim monument in a forgotten place.
