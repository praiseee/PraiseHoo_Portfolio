Zenify (Mobile)

Zenify works as a wellness app designed to support users in their journey towards a better mental and physical health. Provide better understanding of the concept of stress/anxiety, cultivating healthy mechanisms to reduce and cope with such physiological responses. Building on the increased awareness and demand for wellbeing solutions that emerged during the COVID-19 pandemic, Zenify integrates a variety of features to support users in the post-pandemic world remaining relevant at any stage. Zenify aims to provide digital solutions to help users achieve balance in their daily lives.

Software Used

    Figma - Prototyping of mobile application with interactive buttons and effects.
