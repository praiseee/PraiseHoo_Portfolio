Space Cadet (VR)

Space Cadet (VR) is designed to provide an immersive and educational experience where aspiring and active astronauts take on the role of an astronaut landing on the moon. The project enables users to explore the moon's surface, complete critical astronaut tasks such as collecting moon samples, building a lunar base, and learning survival strategies in space. Through this hands-on experience, astronauts will gain valuable training in the unique challenges they will face during space missions, enhancing their preparedness for real-world operations.

Software Used

    Unity - Terrain Building.
    C# - Development of VR game interactions, UI/UX elements, and sprite integration.
