Meet The Ferruccis

Your objective is to investigate a murder that has taken place out of the blue. You are to identify the murderer behind this mysterious crime. As a player, you play an important role as a Detective known as “Callahan”. You are to explore the mansion and its surroundings, gather clues, interrogate suspects, and piece together the evidence to solve the case. Your success hinges on your ability to navigate through a web of deception and uncover hidden motives within the royal family.

Software Used

    Maya - Moodular assets of house including interior and exterior, garden gates and fountain.
    Figma - Mobile prototype to accompany the game.
    Unity - Terrain Building, VFX of smoke, fire and water. Cut scenes and interactive elements for player exploration.
    C# - Programmed player interactions, clue collection, dialogue systems, and logic for suspect interrogation and narrative progression.
    Mixamo - Rigged and animated character models for suspects and the detective, including movement, idle, and interrogation animations.
