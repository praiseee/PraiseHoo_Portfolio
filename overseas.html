Overseas Immersion Program: China

üìç Shenzhen

(17 Apr 2024 - 30 Apr 2024)

I had the incredible opportunity to participate in a two-week Overseas Immersion Programme (OIP) in Shenzhen, China, as part of a cultural and academic exchange with Shenzhen Polytechnic University.

Throughout the programme, we were warmly welcomed by students from Shenzhen Polytechnic University, who brought us on guided tours around their campus and their city. They introduced us to Shenzhen's rich cultural heritage through visits to historical landmarks such as Dapeng Fortress and Nantou Ancient Town. These outings, paired with bonding activities and shared meals, created meaningful opportunities for cross-cultural exchange and genuine friendships.

A key highlight of the trip was the series of company visits to some of Shenzhen's most influential tech innovators. Shenzhen is often referred to as China's "Silicon Valley", where it's home to countless cutting-edge companies shaping the future of technology.

During the trip, we visited several innovative companies such as Han's Laser Institute, UBTECH Robotics, and Narwal Robotics. One of the most memorable company visits was to Tencent, the tech powerhouse behind WeChat. It was fascinating to learn how deeply their technology is embedded into everyday life in China.

Special thanks to Shenzhen Polytechnic University and all the organizing partners for curating such a meaningful and enriching experience!
